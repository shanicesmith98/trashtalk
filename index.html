What is Trash Talk?

Trash Talk is an immersive PC/VR game created by the students of CUNY New York City College of Technology enrolled in Dominika’s Fall 2020 Technical Production course.

While exploring and listening to the environment, help orchestrate and clean the landscape by constructing sound sculptures and acoustic architectural structures.
