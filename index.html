TrashTalk: A VR Exploratorium

What is Trash Talk?

TrashTalk is an immersive multi-media project created by the students of CUNY New York City College of Technology enrolled in Dominika Ksel’s Fall 2020 Technical Production course centered around Climate Justice on a global and local scale. The game was made for PC/VR devices and is intended to spread awareness of climate change with the participation of those currently fighting against the economic and social justice systems.

Gameplay and Instructions

While exploring and listening to the environment, help orchestrate and clean the landscape by constructing sound sculptures and acoustic architectural structures.

  • Walk: WASD/arrow keys
  • Jump: Spacebar
  • Camera view: Move mouse
  • Grab trash: Hold left mouse button
  • Release trash: Release left mouse button
