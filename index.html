Urban Climate Justice: A VR Exploratorium

What is Trash Talk?

Trash Talk is an immersive multi-media project created by the students of CUNY New York City College of Technology enrolled in Dominika Ksel’s Fall 2020 Technical Production course centered around achieving Climate Justice, globally and locally. The game was made for PC/VR devices and is intended to spread awareness of climate change with the participation of those currently fighting against the economic and social justice systems.

While exploring and listening to the environment, help orchestrate and clean the landscape by constructing sound sculptures and acoustic architectural structures.
