What is Trash Talk?

Trash Talk is an immersive PC/VR game created by the students of CUNY New York City College of Technology enrolled in Dominika Ksel’s Fall 2020 Technical Production course centered around achieving Climate Justice, globally and locally.

While exploring and listening to the environment, help orchestrate and clean the landscape by constructing sound sculptures and acoustic architectural structures.
